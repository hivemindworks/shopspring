About Spring

  • What is Spring?

    Spring is a mobile app that enables you to shop and discover more than 800 amazing brands in one place, with free shipping on everything. Every day Spring is filled with new arrivals from brands including Levi’s, Marc Jacobs, Calvin Klein, Opening Ceremony, Steven Alan, Everlane, Beyoncé, Warby Parker, 3.1 Phillip Lim, American Apparel, and hundreds more.
    Spring is designed to be a marketplace where people and brands can connect — when you make a purchase, you are buying directly from the brand, and the brand sends its product directly to you. We just play matchmaker!

  • How can I get Spring?

    Download the Spring app on the Apple App Store and the Google Play Store. Spring is currently available on iPhone, iPad, iPod touch, and Android.

  • Do I have to pay to use Spring?

    Nope! Like the best things in life, Spring is free to join.

  • I'm a brand and I want to sell on Spring — can I join too?

    We'd love to hear from you! Just share some info with us and our Brand Curation team will be in touch.

My account

  • What is guest mode?

    Guest mode is a way to preview Spring without creating an account. You can browse products in guest mode, but you must join Spring to make a purchase.
    And joining Spring gives you all kinds of other wonderful benefits: We’ll send you sale alerts and great recommendations, and your feed will be filled with amazing items from the brands you follow.

  • How do I change my password?

Change your email by editing directly in the email field.

Orders

  • When will my order arrive?

    When you make a purchase on Spring, you are placing an order directly with a brand. Each brand offers its own shipping method, so the answer to “When will my order arrive?” is: It depends.
    When you place an order, you will receive a confirmation email from Spring. The brand will then process your order and ship the item to you directly.
    You can view your order status in the “ME” tab on your iOS device, or in the left menu navigation under “Orders” on your Android device. Select an order to see the shipping method and estimated arrival date. You can also view the estimated arrival date in your confirmation email from Spring.

  • When will you charge my credit card?

    Spring will not charge your credit card until your order is shipped. You may see a pending charge on your account before shipment, but Spring will not capture payment until your order is sent out for delivery.

  • Can I modify my order before it is shipped?

    If you would like to edit your order before it ships, kindly send an email to concierge@shopspring.com and a member of our team will work with the brand to modify your order. If your item has already shipped, Spring Concierge will facilitate a quick and easy return process.

  • How do I cancel my order?

    To cancel your order, please send an email to concierge@shopspring.com and a member of our team will work with the brand to cancel your order before shipment. If your item has already shipped, Spring Concierge will facilitate a quick and easy return process.

  • How do I check out with multiple items?

    On Spring you can purchase only one item at a time. To purchase different items, you must “Swipe to pay” for each one separately.
    If you would like to order more than one of a particular item, you can tap “Review Order” at checkout and increase the quantity.
    If you want to save other products to buy or review later, just double-tap the product image. This will save that product to your “ME” tab on your iOS device, or to your “My Loves” tab on Android. Tap any of your Loves and buy anything you want with a swipe.

  • Where can I find my order number?

    Once you place an order, you can find all your order information — including your order number — in the “My Orders” section of the “ME” tab on your iOS device, or by tapping “Orders” in the left navigation menu on your Android. You will find the order number on the top right hand corner. You can also view the status of your order, the estimated arrival date, the brand’s return policy, and the brand’s contact information. If you have any questions about your order, tap “Support” to send an email to Spring Concierge.

  • I placed an order but didn't receive a confirmation email. Did my order go through?

    Every time you place an order, you will receive a confirmation email from Spring. If you didn’t receive an email, first check your Spam folder. If the email isn’t there, your order may not have been processed.
    You can double-check by going to “My Orders” in the “ME” tab on your iOS device, or by tapping “Orders” in the left navigation menu on your Android device. If your order is there, then everything is great! You can see the status of your order at the top. If you don’t see your order under “My Orders,” we're sorry to say it was not processed. Please contact us and we’ll make things right!

  • I received credits on Spring. How do I apply them to my order?

    Any Spring credits in your account will be automatically applied to your next order. If your credits exceed the total cost of your purchase, the remaining balance will be kept in your account.

Payment

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, Apple Pay, and Google Wallet. We do not currently accept Paypal, Amazon Payments, or other payment providers not listed here.

  • How do I add my credit card?

    You can change your payment information with just three simple steps: 1. Go to the “Me” tab at the bottom right of the app screen 2. Tap the Settings button, located at the top right of the page (it looks like a gear!) 3. Scroll down and tap on Payment Methods, located under Preferences. You will be able to add new credit cards to your profile. You can also add a new card any time you place an order. Once you select an item, hit the "Buy" button and select a size. Then tap the plus sign on the "Payment" line. You can add a new card here, and it will be saved to your Payment Methods in Settings.

  • How do I change my billing details?

    Remove your current credit card Re-add your credit card and enter the appropriate billing address

  • Is my credit card information secure?

    Spring's payment processor is Stripe - a secure PCI Level One compliant service provider. Spring never touches your payment information or holds any sensitive information. Once an order has been placed, Stripe then requests authorization from your bank. Your account will not be charged until your order has shipped.

  • Why was I charged multiple times?

    Spring is partnered with Stripe, a secure PCI level one compliant service provider to process payments. When you place an order, Stripe will request authorization from your bank. You will not be charged until your order has shipped. Stripe will reauthorize your credit card every 6 days until your order ships, which is why you may see a second charge on your account. Not to worry, this pending charge will fall off shortly! Stripe will stop reauthorizing your card as soon as your item ships.

  • When will I receive my refund?

    Because we are a marketplace, brands on Spring set their own return policy. Therefore, the refund processing time may vary by brand. Contact concierge@shopspring.com and we will request an update on your refund status immediately.

  • How do I take advantage of a Spring promotion?

    Promotions are automatically applied at checkout

  • What’s the difference between a Spring credit and promotion?

    Promotions are valid for a limited time only and will automatically apply at checkout Credits are issued by Spring. Spring credits will automatically apply to your next order. If your credits exceed the total cost of your purchase, they will roll over to your next order.

Shipping and returns

  • How much is shipping?

    Free shipping on all orders!

  • Do you ship internationally?

    Currently, Spring only supports shipping within the continental United States and its territories.

  • How do I pay for expedited shipping?

    We do not currently offer the option to expedite shipping; I hope this is not an inconvenience for you! As a marketplace, we allow the brands on Spring to set their own shipping methods, so shipping may vary by brand. You can view each brand's shipping method and estimated delivery date in each post; just tap “Product Info.” Contact concierge@shopspring.com and we will do everything we can to get you your item whenever you need it.

  • What is your return policy?

    Because we are a marketplace, brands on Spring set their own return policy. Therefore, the policy will be different for each brand. Be sure to check the return policy listed in the post by tapping 'Product Details'. You can also see it in orders you have placed in the 'ME' tab on your iPhone, or the left menu navigation on your Android.

  • How do I return my item?

    To return an item, first verify your product's return policy. You can find a brand's return policy in the 'ME' tab under 'Order Details' on your iPhone, or by tapping 'Orders' in the left menu navigation on your Android. Should you have any questions about the return process, please email Spring Concierge and a member of our team will respond to you promptly. We’re here Monday to Friday, 8AM EST to 8PM EST.

  • Did you receive my return?

    Because we are a marketplace, your item is returned directly to the brand. Please contact concierge@shopspring.com and we will request a status on your return.

  • Can I return an item that was purchased as Final Sale?

    Brands set their own return policies- returns are not final sale unless noted by the brand

  • How do I exchange my order?

    Our Concierge team is happy to facilitate the exchange process. Simply send us an email at concierge@shopspring.com with your exchange details and we will take care of it.

How to use spring

  • How can I find and follow brands on Spring?

    This is one of our favorite features. There are hundreds of brands to love and discover in three easy steps: BRANDS 1. For iPhone, tap the ‘Brands’ tab at the bottom of your Spring screen. For Android, tap the left menu navigation and select ‘Brands.’ 2. Search by brand name or explore brands by category. 3. When you see a brand that you want to follow, simply tap the plus button next to the brand's name. If you want to see more from any brand, tap on the brand name to visit their Spring Shop. Tap the 'Follow' button in the brand's Spring Shop to add them to your personalized feed BROWSE You can also discover new brands in the Browse tab, where you can explore by category from all brands on Spring - whether or not you follow them. You can tap into any post to learn more about the product and tap the brand logo to visit their Spring Shop and 'Follow' them. If you can’t find a brand you are looking for, they might not yet be Spring. But we'd love to know about them! Send an email to brandrequest@shopspring.com - discovering new brands is one of our favorite things to do.

  • How can I find a specific product?

    There are a few ways to find a product on Spring: 1. If you know the brand name, tap the 'Brands' tab and search for the brand by name. Click into their Spring Shop by tapping on their logo, and browse all the products to find the one you're looking for. 2. You can also browse products by category. Simply go to the 'Browse' tab and select from Women’s, Men’s, Lifestyle or Beauty, then select the product category and browse all products available in that category. If you don’t see the product you're looking for, the brand may not have posted it on Spring. Shoot us a note at Spring Concierge with the brand name, product name, and any other information and we’ll try to track it down for you!

  • Can I save items for later? Where can I see them?

    Simply love any item on Spring and it’s saved to view later. Just tap the Heart icon under the product image or double tap the image itself to love an item. To see your loved items you've saved, tap the 'ME' section on your iPhone, or the 'My Loves' tab on your Android. You can use this to save your items for later or collect all your favorites in one place.

  • How do I buy my first item?

    Select the item you want to purchase, enter your shipping/billing/payment information, swipe to pay, be on the lookout for an email from Spring confirming your purchase. Browse features Scroll through categories and tap on Refine in the upper right-hand corner to search by smaller, sub-categories You can also browse products by category. Simply go to the 'Browse' tab and select from Women’s, Men’s, Lifestyle or Beauty, then select the product category and browse all products available in that category.
